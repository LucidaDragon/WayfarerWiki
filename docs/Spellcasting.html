Spellcasting

Terminology

Mana is an ethereal substance produced by souls that powers all magic. The term mana is also the name of the mana unit M̈, a unit of measurement for ~100 joules of mana substance. The following formulas provide energy in joules for simplicity. If energy in M̈ is desired, the result should be divided by 100.

Conjuration

Conjuration is the creation of material objects by projecting mana from the Unreal into the world. The cost of conjuring an object is proportional to its volume. $$E_{conjure} = ρDV$$ (E = ρDV) Where ρ is the Solange constant 500,000 m2/s2, D is the density of the conjured object in kg/m3, and V is the volume of the object in m3.

Dense Objects

Conjured objects can be up to 10,000 kg/m3 in density. If a greater density is desired, additional energy must be used to change it. $$E_{density} = \Delta DVs$$ (E = DVs) Where s is the measured constant ~89,875,517,900,000,000 m2/s2, D is the desired additional density in kg/m3, and V is the volume of the object in m3.

Hot or Cold Objects

Conjured objects inherit the temperature of their surroundings. If a different temperature is desired, additional energy must be used to change it. $$E_{heat} = cDV|\Delta T|$$ (E = cDVT) Where c is the measured constant ~1,125 J/kgK, T is the temperature change in celsius, D is the density of the conjured object in kg/m3, and V is the volume of the object in m3.

Moving Objects

Conjured objects inherit the velocity of their casting source. If a different velocity is desired, additional energy must be used to change it. $$E_{kinetic} = \frac{1000DVv^{2}}{2}$$ (E = (1000*DV(v^2))/2) Where v is the desired velocity in m/s, D is the density of the conjured object in kg/m3, and V is the volume of the object in m3.

If a specific distance of travel is desired, an extension of the above formula may be used instead. $$E_{kinetic} = \frac{1000DVdg}{2}$$ (E = (1000*DVdg)/2) Where d is the desired range in m, g is the local gravitational acceleration in m/s2, D is the density of the conjured object in kg/m3, and V is the volume of the object in m3.

Derivation of ρ (Solange Constant)

$$ρ = ad$$ (ρ = ad) Where a is the measured acceleration of ~1,000 m/s2, and d is the measured depth of ~500 m.

Hide & Reveal

Objects can be magically hidden by expending energy to partially project them into the Unreal. Revealing a magically hidden object takes an equivalent amount of energy to unproject it. In both cases the object can not exceed 10,000 kg/m3 in density. $$E_{hide} = \frac{ρDV}{2}$$ (E = (ρDV)/2) Where ρ is the Solange constant 500,000 m2/s2, D is the density of the conjured object in kg/m3, and V is the volume of the object in m3.

Enchantment

A spell can be attached to an object through enchantment, allowing it to be repeatedly cast until its energy source is depleated. Enchantments use an additional ~10% of their casting energy to be created and will need to be recast if the available energy falls below the casting energy.
